# England (2 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### disinfection


a process of water treatment to remove, or render harmless to health, every pathogenic micro-organism and pathogenic parasite


  - **Private Water Supplies (England) Regulations 2016, Regulation 2(1) [TIER 2]**


---


### private water supply


a supply of water other than a supply provided directly by a water undertaker or water supply licensee


  - **Private Water Supplies (England) Regulations 2016, Regulation 2(1) [TIER 2]**

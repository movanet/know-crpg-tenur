# Singapore (8 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### Apparatus


Any water apparatus, equipment or accessory and includes all apparatus, devices and fittings in which pipes are used or of which they form a part.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**


---


### Connecting pipe


A pipe from a water main to the meter position, used for delivering water to individual consumers.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**


---


### consumer


A person who is supplied with water or whose premises are for the time being connected for the purpose of a supply of water with any system of supply.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**


---


### Discharge pipe


A pipe that enables any sewage to be conveyed away from premises into the public sewerage system.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**


---


### Public sewerage system


Sewerage infrastructure designed to convey sewage (not combined with storm water) from individual premises to treatment facilities.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**


---


### Sewage (under SDA 1999)


Wastewater from domestic and commercial sources including fecal matter, waste water, and other effluents that require treatment and disposal through sewerage systems.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**


---


### Used water


Water that has been used in domestic, commercial, or industrial processes and requires treatment before disposal or reuse.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water (under PUA 2001)


Fresh water suitable for drinking, domestic use, and other permitted purposes supplied through a public water system.


  - **Public Utilities Act 2001 (Chapter 261) / Sewerage and Drainage Act 1999 (Chapter 294), PUA 2001 s.2 / SDA 1999 s.2, (2001) [TIER 2]**

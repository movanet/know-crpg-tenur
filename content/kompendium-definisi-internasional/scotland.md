# Scotland (6 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### Core functions


All statutory functions of Scottish Water, but excluding its new general power under section 25(1) and the ancillary power under section 25(2).


  - **Water Industry (Scotland) Act 2002 / Water Services etc. (Scotland) Act 2005, 2002 Act s.2 / 2005 Act s.2, (2002) [TIER 2]**


---


### Public sewerage system


Infrastructure for providing sewerage services to the public in Scotland, including collection, treatment, and disposal of sewage.


  - **Water Industry (Scotland) Act 2002 / Water Services etc. (Scotland) Act 2005, 2002 Act s.2 / 2005 Act s.2, (2002) [TIER 2]**


---


### Public water supply system


Infrastructure for providing water supply services to the public in Scotland, including source management, treatment, and distribution networks.


  - **Water Industry (Scotland) Act 2002 / Water Services etc. (Scotland) Act 2005, 2002 Act s.2 / 2005 Act s.2, (2002) [TIER 2]**


---


### Scottish Water


The public water authority established under the Water Industry (Scotland) Act 2002 to provide water and sewerage services in Scotland, replacing the North, South and East of Scotland Water Authorities.


  - **Water Industry (Scotland) Act 2002 / Water Services etc. (Scotland) Act 2005, 2002 Act s.2 / 2005 Act s.2, (2002) [TIER 2]**


---

### the Groundwater Directive


Directive 2006/118/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council on the protection of groundwater against pollution and deterioration


  - **Water Environment and Water Services (Scotland) Act 2003, s.2(8) [TIER 2]**

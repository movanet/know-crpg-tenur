# Portugal (3 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### Sewerage services (Serviços de saneamento de águas residuais)


Urban wastewater services including collection, treatment and disposal of sewage, regulated by ERSAR under integrated regulatory authority.


  - **Lei n. 10/2014 (ERSAR Estatutos) / Lei n. 58/2005 (Lei da Água), Lei 10/2014 Artigo 2 (ERSAR governance) / Lei 58/2005, (2014) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water and Waste Services Regulation Authority (ERSAR)


The Portuguese independent regulatory authority with dual mandates: regulation of drinking water supply services, urban wastewater management services and municipal waste management services; and national authority for drinking water quality.


  - **Lei n. 10/2014 (ERSAR Estatutos) / Lei n. 58/2005 (Lei da Água), Lei 10/2014 Artigo 2 (ERSAR governance) / Lei 58/2005, (2014) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water supply services (Serviços de abastecimento de água)


Public water supply services including extraction, treatment, storage, distribution and supply of water suitable for human consumption, regulated by ERSAR (Entidade Reguladora dos Serviços de Águas e Resíduos).


  - **Lei n. 10/2014 (ERSAR Estatutos) / Lei n. 58/2005 (Lei da Água), Lei 10/2014 Artigo 2 (ERSAR governance) / Lei 58/2005, (2014) [TIER 2]**

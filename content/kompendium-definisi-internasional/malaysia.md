# Malaysia (7 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### Communal septic tank


A septic tank which treats sewage from two or more premises through a common internal sewerage piping.


  - **National Water Services Commission Act 2006 (Act 654) / Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655), Act 654 s.2 / Act 655 s.2, (2006) [TIER 2]**


---


### Private sewerage system


A sewerage system other than a public sewerage system, typically serving individual premises or limited groups.


  - **National Water Services Commission Act 2006 (Act 654) / Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655), Act 654 s.2 / Act 655 s.2, (2006) [TIER 2]**


---


### Public sewerage system


A sewerage system owned and operated by a public authority or licensee for collection and treatment of sewage from multiple premises.


  - **National Water Services Commission Act 2006 (Act 654) / Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655), Act 654 s.2 / Act 655 s.2, (2006) [TIER 2]**


---


### Sewerage services


The collection, carriage, treatment and disposal of sewage or sewage sludge, and includes the operation and maintenance of the sewerage system and septic tank effluents.


  - **National Water Services Commission Act 2006 (Act 654) / Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655), Act 654 s.2 / Act 655 s.2, (2006) [TIER 2]**


---


### Sewerage system


Infrastructure including collection pipes, treatment facilities, and disposal systems for managing sewage and sewerage services.


  - **National Water Services Commission Act 2006 (Act 654) / Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655), Act 654 s.2 / Act 655 s.2, (2006) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water supply services


Water treatment derived from the watercourse and the distribution and supply of water treated to users and includes the operation and maintenance of the water supply system.


  - **National Water Services Commission Act 2006 (Act 654) / Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655), Act 654 s.2 / Act 655 s.2, (2006) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water supply system


Infrastructure including sources, treatment facilities, distribution networks, and related equipment for providing water supply services.


  - **National Water Services Commission Act 2006 (Act 654) / Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655), Act 654 s.2 / Act 655 s.2, (2006) [TIER 2]**

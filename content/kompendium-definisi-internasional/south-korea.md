# South Korea (3 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### processed water


Water properly treated for drinking or industrial use


  - **Water Supply and Waterworks Installation Act, South Korea, Article 3 [TIER 2]**


---


### waterworks


Facilities with pipelines providing raw or processed water


  - **Water Supply and Waterworks Installation Act, South Korea, Article 3 [TIER 2]**


---


### wide-area waterworks


Serves two or more local governments


  - **Water Supply and Waterworks Installation Act, South Korea, Article 3 [TIER 2]**

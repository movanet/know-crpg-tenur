# Queensland, Australia (2 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### service provider


an entity that applies for and receives registration to supply water or sewerage services, including local governments and water authorities that own infrastructure


  - **Water Supply (Safety and Reliability) Act 2008, s.1 and Schedule 3, (2008) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water Supply


includes water harvesting or collection (e.g. dams, weirs, bores and direct extraction from watercourses) and bulk water service providers


  - **Water Supply (Safety and Reliability) Act 2008, s.1 and Schedule 3, (2008) [TIER 2]**

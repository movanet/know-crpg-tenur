# Australia  Victoria (8 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### Approved Great Ocean Road Strategic Framework Plan


An equivalent interstate right that has been approved under section 33AG as a right under which the holder may take water in Victoria.


  - **Water Act 1989, Part 1, s.3 (Definitions), (1989) [TIER 2]**


---


### Approved Place


A place approved under a general place of take approval as a place from which water may be taken.


  - **Water Act 1989, Part 1, s.3 (Definitions), (1989) [TIER 2]**


---


### Aquifer


A geological structure or formation or an artificial land fill permeated or capable of being permeated permanently or intermittently with water.


  - **Water Act 1989, Part 1, s.3 (Definitions), (1989) [TIER 2]**


---


### Drinking Water


Water that is intended for human consumption or for purposes connected with human consumption, such as preparation of food or making of ice for consumption or for preservation of unpackaged food, whether or not the water is used for other purposes.


  - **Safe Drinking Water Act 2003, s.3 (Definitions), (2003) [TIER 2]**


---


### Regulated Water


Water that is not intended for drinking but could be mistaken as drinking water. The Minister for Health can declare a water supply as regulated water to protect public health.


  - **Safe Drinking Water Act 2003, s.3 (Definitions), (2003) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water Quality Standard


A quality standard specified for drinking water by regulations made for the purposes of section 17.


  - **Safe Drinking Water Act 2003, s.3 (Definitions), (2003) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water Supplier


Any entity that supplies drinking water or regulated water to the public, including authorities within the Water Act 1989, Parks Victoria, Alpine Resorts Victoria, or any other person or body declared by regulations to be a water supplier.


  - **Safe Drinking Water Act 2003, s.3 (Definitions), (2003) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water Supply Premises


Any premises that has been, is being, or is to be used in connection with the supply, storage, treatment, transport, harvesting, sale or analysis of drinking water, other than premises that only stores or treats drinking water for the purposes of those using the premises.


  - **Safe Drinking Water Act 2003, s.3 (Definitions), (2003) [TIER 2]**

# Australia  Queensland (5 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### Drinking Water Service


A service for supplying water suitable for human consumption.


  - **Water Supply (Safety and Reliability) Act 2008, Schedule 3 (Dictionary), (2008) [TIER 2]**


---


### Manufactured Water


Water, including desalinated or recycled water or any substance resulting from the production.


  - **Water Supply (Safety and Reliability) Act 2008, Schedule 3 (Dictionary), (2008) [TIER 2]**


---


### Recycled Water


Water treated for reuse from various sources.


  - **Water Supply (Safety and Reliability) Act 2008, Schedule 3 (Dictionary), (2008) [TIER 2]**


---


### Service Provider Water Restriction


Limits on water volume, usage hours, or methods imposed by providers.


  - **Water Supply (Safety and Reliability) Act 2008, Schedule 3 (Dictionary), (2008) [TIER 2]**


---


### Water Service Provider


Entity registered to supply water or sewerage services.


  - **Water Supply (Safety and Reliability) Act 2008, Schedule 3 (Dictionary), (2008) [TIER 2]**

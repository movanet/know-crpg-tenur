# France (3 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### assainissement (wastewater treatment/sanitation)


treatment of wastewater (collective or non-collective sanitation, industrial treatment facilities) mandatory before discharge into natural environments


  - **Code de l'environnement (Environmental Code), L210-1 et suivants, (2000) [TIER 2]**


---


### eau potable (drinking water)


water subject to accessibility principles established in the Code; everyone has the right to access drinking water under economically acceptable conditions


  - **Code de l'environnement (Environmental Code), L210-1 et suivants, (2000) [TIER 2]**


---


### eau ressource (water resource)


part of the common heritage framework; usage belongs to everyone subject to laws, regulations and previously established rights


  - **Code de l'environnement (Environmental Code), L210-1 et suivants, (2000) [TIER 2]**

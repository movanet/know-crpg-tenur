# Ireland (4 definitions)

[Back to Index](.)



### abstraction


the doing of anything whereby any of that water is removed from that source of water, whether temporarily or permanently, including anything whereby the water is so removed for the purpose of being transferred to another source of water


  - **Water Services Act 2007 (Ireland), s.2 (Interpretation) [TIER 2]**


---


### groundwater


all water below the land surface that is not in a pipe or similarly contained


  - **Water Services Act 2007 (Ireland), s.2 [TIER 2]**


---


### trade effluent


effluent from any premises used for carrying on any trade or industry, other than domestic waste water


  - **Water Services Act 2007 (Ireland), s.2 [TIER 2]**


---


### waste water


sewage, storm water or other effluent discharged by a person to a drain, service connection or sewer


  - **Water Services Act 2007 (Ireland), s.2 [TIER 2]**

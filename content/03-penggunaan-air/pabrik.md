# Pabrik

> "Di sini udah banyak pabrik. Daerah aku lumayan dekat sama PT Suton. Jadi agak tercemarlah" - Ulia[^1]

### 4.3 Theme 2: Physical Barriers to Water Collection

**Adaptive Strategies:**

**Pak Wartika (leg amputation, 2 crutches):**
> "Diikat di kepala galonya. Kalau enggak digendong, ya kepaksa pelan-pelan ditenteng pakai tangan"

**Pak Sutarno (arm amputation):**
> "Kesulitan sih pasti ada. Lebih kecapainya kan. Tapi dengan satu tangan ya alhamdulillah bisa dilaksanakan semua"

**Pak Sutarno's solution:** Purchased washing machine after 5 months (economic barrier for many)

### 4.4 Theme 3: Social Exclusion from Governance

**Key Quotes:**
> "Never invited, never involved... Sometimes people belittle you - 'what do you understand'... I feel ostracized because I'm disabled" - Pak Wartika

> "Most people here still think disabled people should just stay at home, no need to join meetings. Here it still follows patriarchal culture" - Ulia


[^1]: Cimanuk FAO Water Scarcity Program. (2025). *Comprehensive Water Tenure Thematic Analysis*, Section: 4.2 Theme 1: Water Source Disparity.

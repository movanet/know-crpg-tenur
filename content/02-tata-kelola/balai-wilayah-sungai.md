# Balai Wilayah Sungai

*Balai Besar Wilayah Sungai* /[[bbws]] (Large River Basin Organization) and *Balai Wilayah Sungai/BWS* (River Basin Organization) have been established as a technical implementing unit of Ministry of Public Works which is tasked with management of water resources in river area.30
